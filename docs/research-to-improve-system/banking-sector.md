# 1️⃣ NAICS Classification (U.S. Standard)

## 🏦 Commercial Banking

**NAICS 522110 – Commercial Banking**

* Retail Banking
* Commercial / Corporate Banking
* Regional Banks
* Community Banks

## 🏠 Savings Institutions

**NAICS 522120 – Savings Institutions**

* Thrifts
* Savings & Loan Associations

## 🤝 Credit Unions

**NAICS 522130 – Credit Unions**

## 💳 Credit Intermediation (Non-Bank / Specialty)

**NAICS 522210 – Credit Card Issuing**
**NAICS 522220 – Sales Financing**
**NAICS 522291 – Consumer Lending**

* Specialty Finance
* Non-bank lenders

## 🏦 Investment Banking & Securities

**NAICS 523110 – Investment Banking & Securities Dealing**

* Investment Banking
* Sales & Trading

## 📈 Asset Management

**NAICS 523920 – Portfolio Management**

* Asset Management
* Wealth Management

## 🏛 Custody & Clearing

**NAICS 523991 – Trust, Fiduciary & Custody Activities**

## 🏡 Mortgage Banking

**NAICS 522292 – Real Estate Credit**

## 💻 FinTech

Typically classified under:

* **522320 – Financial Transactions Processing**
* **518210 – Data Processing / Hosting**
  (depending on business model)
