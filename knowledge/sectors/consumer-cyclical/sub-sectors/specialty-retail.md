# Specialty Retail

## Overview

Specialty retailers operate focused store concepts in specific categories: pet supplies (Chewy CHWY), electronics (Best Buy BBY), hobby/craft (Michaels, private), home goods (TJX, Ross ROST, Burlington BURL), sporting goods (Dick's DKS, Academy Sports ASO), beauty (Ulta ULTA, Sally Beauty SBH). Business models range from off-price (TJX, ROST -- treasure hunt, no e-commerce dependency) to category killers (BBY, DKS). Off-price retailers have been secular winners, gaining share from department stores. Key differentiators: curation, expertise, or price advantage that justifies the physical store visit.

## Valuation Approach

- **Primary method:** EV/EBITDA 10-18x. Off-price (TJX, ROST) at premium (16-20x). Category killers at 10-14x.
- **Common pitfalls:** (1) Not distinguishing secular winners (off-price) from secular losers (electronics in commodity categories). (2) Ignoring membership/loyalty program value.

## Turnaround Precedents

| Company | Period | Situation | Actions Taken | Outcome | Timeline | Stock Recovery |
|---------|--------|-----------|--------------|---------|----------|----------------|
| Best Buy | 2012-2016 | "Amazon showroom" thesis, revenue declining, CEO departed | New CEO Hubert Joly: price matching, services pivot, cost cuts, vendor partnerships | Successful | 3-4 years | Stock from $12 (2012) to $45+ (2016) |
| Dick's Sporting Goods | 2017-2020 | Online competition, gun sales controversy, margin pressure | Exited firearms category, private label growth, experiential stores | Successful | 3 years | Stock from $25 (2017) to $70+ (2021) |
| GameStop | 2019-present | Physical game sales declining, digital distribution threat | Meme stock mania (not operational turnaround), e-commerce pivot attempted | Failed (as operational turnaround) | Ongoing | Stock driven by speculation, not fundamentals |

**Pattern:** Specialty retail turnarounds succeed when management creates a defensible reason for the store to exist (services, experience, curation). Best Buy is the gold standard. 2 of 3 operational turnarounds succeeded. Base rate ~65%.
