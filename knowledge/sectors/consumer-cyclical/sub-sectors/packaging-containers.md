# Packaging & Containers

## Overview

Packaging companies (Ball BLL, Sealed Air SEE, Berry Global BERY, Sonoco SON, Graphic Packaging GPK) produce packaging materials (plastic, metal, paper) for consumer and industrial applications. Revenue is driven by end-market demand (food/beverage, e-commerce, industrial) and raw material costs. Margins are moderate (EBITDA 15-22%) with cost pass-through mechanisms. The sector faces sustainability pressures (plastic reduction, recycling mandates). Note: GICS classifies this under Materials, but FMP places it in Consumer Cyclical. This file is included for FMP screener compatibility.

## Valuation Approach

- **Primary method:** EV/EBITDA 8-12x. Stable demand but limited growth.
- **Common pitfalls:** (1) Not adjusting for commodity input cost cycles. (2) Ignoring plastic regulation risk.

## Risk Profile

- **Default risk type:** Operational
- **Cyclicality:** Low to Moderate (packaging demand is non-discretionary for food/beverage)
- **Regulatory discretion:** Moderate (sustainability regulations evolving)
- **Binary trigger frequency:** Rare
