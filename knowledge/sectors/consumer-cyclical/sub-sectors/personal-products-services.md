# Personal Products & Services

## Overview

Personal products and services companies include beauty/personal care (Coty COTY, Edgewell Personal Care EPC), pet services, salon/spa chains, and consumer services. Revenue driven by consumer spending on personal care, grooming, and wellness. The beauty segment benefits from "lipstick effect" resilience in downturns. Many companies have shifted to DTC and subscription models. The sector overlaps with Consumer Staples for essential personal care but FMP classifies discretionary personal services here.

## Valuation Approach

- **Primary method:** EV/EBITDA 10-16x. Prestige beauty brands at premium (14-18x). Mass personal care at 8-12x.
- **Common pitfalls:** Ignoring brand portfolio quality differences within the same company.

**Pattern:** Personal care turnarounds depend on brand relevance and leverage management. 1 of 1 partially recovered. Base rate ~50%.

## Risk Profile

- **Default risk type:** Operational
- **Cyclicality:** Low to Moderate
- **Regulatory discretion:** Moderate (FDA cosmetics regulation expanding via MoCRA 2022)
- **Binary trigger frequency:** Rare (product safety recalls are the main risk)

### Kill Factors
